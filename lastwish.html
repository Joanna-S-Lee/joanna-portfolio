Book recommendation: The Last Wish

March 11th 2021

I highly recommend The Witcher series. And The Last Wish by Andrzej Sapkowski is a good place to start. A collection of short stories introducing you to the world and its characters. I went into the series expecting a good fantasy treat, but I did not realize it would be so witty as well.
