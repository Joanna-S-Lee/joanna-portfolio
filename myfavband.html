My favourite band that I never got to see live

March 14th 2021

The Civil Wars is one of my all-time favourite bands. Their music is very stripped down which highlights the harmonizing of the duo. It is then extremely unfortunate that they broke up before I could see them live. They only produced two albums. While both parties went on to make great music solo, the what ifs never stop whenever I give their albums a spin.
